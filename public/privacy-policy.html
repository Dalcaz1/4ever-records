Privacy Policy

4 Ever Memories Records — Last updated: June 8, 2026
Covers: 4evermemoriesrecordstore.com and Find Your Tunes (FYT)

This Privacy Policy applies to all products and services operated by 4 Ever Memories Records ("we," "us," or "our"), including the 4 Ever Memories Records website at 4evermemoriesrecordstore.com and the Find Your Tunes (FYT) record scanning and pricing application. By using any of our products, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Our Products

4evermemoriesrecordstore.com

A free public website where customers can browse our record store inventory, view items for sale, and contact us. No purchase is made through the website — all in-store transactions are handled separately via Square at our physical location.

Find Your Tunes (FYT)

A subscription-based mobile and web application that uses AI to identify vinyl records and other music formats from photos and provide real-time market pricing. Available at findyourtunes.vercel.app and through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

2. Information We Collect

4 Ever Memories Records Website

  • Contact form submissions. If you use our contact form, we collect the information you voluntarily provide — such as your name, email address, and message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Basic usage data. We may receive standard web server logs including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for technical operation and security only.
  • PWA install tracking. If you install our website as a Progressive Web App, we record that installation in aggregate form to understand how many users have installed the app. No personal information is collected as part of this.

Find Your Tunes (FYT)

  • Account information. When you create a FYT account, we collect your email address and maintain a record of your subscription tier and account status.
  • Camera and photos. When you scan a record, you grant temporary access to your device camera. Images are captured and transmitted to our AI identification service. Images are processed for identification and are not stored permanently after the scan is complete.
  • Scan data. Information about the records you scan — including artist, title, label, format, and estimated value — may be retained in aggregate form to improve our pricing accuracy over time.
  • Subscription and billing data. Payment information is processed by Stripe and/or Apple/Google. We do not store your credit card number or full payment details. We retain records of your subscription status and payment history for account management.
  • Usage data. We collect basic information about how you use the app, including scan counts and feature usage, to improve the product and enforce subscription limits.
  • Device information. We may receive basic technical information about your device and browser to ensure compatibility. This does not include your name, precise location, or contact details.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect precise location or GPS data.
  • We do not access your photo library or camera roll — only photos you actively take within the app.
  • We do not collect information about your contacts, messages, or other apps.
  • We do not track you across other websites or applications.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our products and services.
  • To identify records and provide pricing information in FYT.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To respond to your contact form inquiries.
  • To improve pricing accuracy using aggregated, anonymized scan data.
  • To monitor app performance and fix technical issues.
  • To enforce subscription tier limits and prevent abuse.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services. Each processes data under its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic Claude API — Record images are sent to Anthropic's API for AI-powered identification. See anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Discogs — We query Discogs for release information and pricing data. See discogs.com/privacy.
  • eBay — We query eBay's API for marketplace pricing. See eBay Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube / Google — We use the YouTube Data API to surface music videos. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing for FYT subscriptions. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Apple / Google — In-app purchases on iOS and Android are processed by Apple and Google respectively under their own terms.
  • Supabase — Our database infrastructure. Data stored on servers in the United States. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Vercel — Our web hosting provider. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Square — In-store payment processing at 4 Ever Memories Records physical location. See Square Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We share data with third parties only as described in Section 5, and only as necessary to operate our services.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. FYT account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Aggregated scan data may be retained indefinitely to improve pricing accuracy. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

8. Camera Permission (FYT)

FYT requests camera access solely to scan physical records. We access your camera only when you actively initiate a scan. We do not access your camera at any other time and do not access your photo library.

9. Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including encrypted HTTPS connections and secure database storage. No method of internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or export data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

12. California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, please contact us.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our products after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
